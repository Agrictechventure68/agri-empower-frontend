    About Us

    Agri_Empower is a digital smart farming platform focused on training, disease identification, access to input suppliers, logistics, and market connections for farmers in Africa and beyond.

    Our team is made up of agricultural experts, software engineers, and rural development advocates passionate about bridging the digital divide in farming through data, design, and inclusiveness.
