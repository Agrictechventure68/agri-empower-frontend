Cash Crop (Perennial Tree Crop)

Overview

Cashew is a major perennial cash crop cultivated for its nutritious nuts and valuable by-products used in food, pharmaceutical, and industrial sectors. It is one of the most important export crops in tropical regions, generating income for millions of smallholder farmers.

Cashew farming combines long-term plantation investment with strong market demand. With proper orchard management, processing, and market linkages, cashew offers high profitability and employment opportunities across the value chain.

Production Systems (Overview)

Cashew is produced under smallholder, cooperative, and commercial plantation systems. Smallholders dominate production, while processors and exporters drive value addition and international trade.
