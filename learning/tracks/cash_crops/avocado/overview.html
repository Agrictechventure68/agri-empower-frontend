Overview

Avocado is a high-value perennial cash crop cultivated for its nutritious fruits used in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries. With rising global demand, avocado farming offers strong income potential for small, medium, and commercial-scale farmers.

Uses & Importance

  • Fresh fruit consumption
  • Oil extraction
  • Cosmetics and skincare products
  • Export agribusiness
