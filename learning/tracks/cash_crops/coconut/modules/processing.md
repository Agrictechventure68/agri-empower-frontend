
# Coconut Processing & Value Addition

This module covers how coconuts can be transformed into value-added
products like copra, oil, desiccated coconut, and fiber-based items.

---

## FOUNDATION LEVEL  
### Basic Processing Opportunities
- Copra drying and storage
- Extracting coconut water for local consumption
- Using coconut husks for compost or small crafts

### Simple Processing Steps
- Husking coconuts manually
- Shelling and grating kernels
- Sun-drying copra for small-scale use

### Safety & Quality Awareness
- Wash hands and tools
- Keep work area clean
- Avoid contaminated water or surfaces

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation

---

## INTERMEDIATE LEVEL  
### Improved Processing Techniques
- Mechanical huskers and graters
- Controlled drying methods for copra
- Making coconut oil using traditional presses

### Quality Control
- Sorting coconuts by maturity and quality
- Cleaning and grading processed products
- Basic hygiene during processing

### Small-Scale Product Development
- Coconut-based snacks
- Simple coconut milk extraction
- Craft items from husk and shell

> **Curriculum Level:** Intermediate

---

## ADVANCED LEVEL  
### Commercial Processing
- Mechanical oil presses and extraction systems
- Desiccated coconut production
- Coir fiber processing

### Processing Efficiency
- Batch planning and workflow optimization
- Reducing post-processing losses
- Maintaining consistent product quality

### Quality Standards
- Grading copra or oil for sale
- Packaging for transport and extended shelf life
- Compliance with food safety regulations

> **Curriculum Level:** Advanced

---

## SPECIALIZATION LEVEL  
### Enterprise Opportunities
- Large-scale coconut oil production
- Export-quality desiccated coconut
- Coir products for industrial use

### Value Chain Integration
- Linking farms with processing plants
- Supply chain planning and logistics
- Coordinating harvest, processing, and sales

### Employment Creation
- Processing plant management
- Technical training for workers
- Advisory services for farmers and cooperatives

> **Curriculum Level:** Specialization

---

## Expected Learning Outcomes
Learners should be able to:
- Identify processing opportunities for coconut
- Apply safe and efficient processing methods
- Maintain quality and hygiene standards
- Engage with commercial supply chains
