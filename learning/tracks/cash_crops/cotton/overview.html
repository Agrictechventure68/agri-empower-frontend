Overview

Cotton is a major industrial cash crop cultivated for its natural fiber, widely used in textile manufacturing and related industries. It also provides valuable by-products such as cottonseed oil and animal feed.

Cotton plays a critical role in rural employment, agro-industrial development, and export earnings. With improved varieties and good agronomic practices, cotton farming offers strong income potential for smallholders and commercial producers.
