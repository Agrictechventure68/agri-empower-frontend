Overview

Rubber is a perennial industrial crop cultivated primarily for its latex, which is processed into natural rubber used in tires, footwear, medical supplies, and numerous industrial products.

Rubber farming supports long-term income generation due to its extended productive lifespan. It plays a vital role in rural employment, agro-industry, and export-driven economies, especially in tropical regions.
