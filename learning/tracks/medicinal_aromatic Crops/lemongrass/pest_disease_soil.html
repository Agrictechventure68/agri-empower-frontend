Pest, Disease & Soil Health

Lemongrass is relatively resistant to pests and diseases, making it suitable for low-chemical and organic farming systems.

  • Common pests: Grasshoppers, stem borers (minor)
  • Diseases: Leaf blight, fungal root rot (under poor drainage)
  • Prevention: Proper spacing and drainage
  • Soil health: Compost, mulching, crop rotation

Biosecurity measures focus on clean planting materials and field sanitation rather than heavy pesticide use.
