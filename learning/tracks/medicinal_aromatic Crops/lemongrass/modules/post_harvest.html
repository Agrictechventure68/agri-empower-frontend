Harvesting & Post-Harvest Handling

Lemongrass is harvested 3–4 months after planting and subsequently every 6–8 weeks depending on growth conditions.

  • Harvest stage: Before flowering for high oil content
  • Method: Cut leaves 10–15 cm above ground
  • Drying: Shade-drying for tea and storage
  • Storage: Cool, dry, well-ventilated environment

Proper handling preserves aroma, oil yield, and medicinal quality.
