Pest, Disease & Soil Health

Moringa is relatively resilient to pests and diseases, making it suitable for organic and low-input farming systems.

  • Common pests: Caterpillars, aphids, mites
  • Diseases: Root rot and leaf spot under poor drainage
  • Control: Field sanitation and biological control
  • Soil health: Organic manure, mulching, and compost application

Good drainage, healthy soils, and proper spacing are the primary biosecurity measures for sustainable moringa production.
