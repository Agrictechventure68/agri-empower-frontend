Agribusiness & Markets

Moringa has strong demand in health, nutrition, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical markets due to its exceptional nutrient profile.

  • Local sales of fresh leaves and powder
  • Supplying herbal and nutraceutical companies
  • Cosmetic and pharmaceutical raw material markets
  • Export opportunities for certified moringa products

Branding, quality assurance, and cooperative processing improve competitiveness and long-term profitability.
