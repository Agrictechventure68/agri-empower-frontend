Medicinal & Aromatic Crop (Spice)

Overview

Turmeric (Curcuma longa) is a high-value medicinal and spice crop widely cultivated for its underground rhizomes. It is used extensively in food, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and traditional medicine due to its strong anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

Turmeric farming is suitable for smallholders and commercial producers. With rising global demand for natural health products, turmeric offers strong income, processing, and export opportunities.
