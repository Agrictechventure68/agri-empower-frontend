Production Overview

Sheep and goat production is a resilient livestock enterprise suitable for smallholder and commercial systems. Proper management improves growth, reproduction, and profitability.

Production Systems

  • Extensive (free-range)
  • Semi-intensive
  • Intensive (stall-fed)

Housing & Environment

  • Dry, well-ventilated housing
  • Protection from rain, heat, and predators
  • Good drainage and sanitation

Feeding & Nutrition

  • Natural pasture and browse
  • Crop residues and fodder
  • Mineral supplements and clean water

Breeding & Reproduction

Selection of healthy breeding stock, controlled mating, and proper care of pregnant females improve kidding and lambing outcomes.
