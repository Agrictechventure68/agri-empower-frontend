Overview

Sheep and goat farming involves the rearing of small ruminants for meat, milk, skin, manure, and breeding purposes. These animals are hardy, adaptable to diverse climates, and suitable for smallholder as well as commercial production systems.

Due to their ability to utilize a wide range of feeds and thrive under low-input systems, sheep and goats play a critical role in food security, rural livelihoods, and income generation across Africa.

Major Products & Uses

  • Meat (mutton and chevon)
  • Milk (especially goat milk)
  • Skin and hides
  • Breeding stock
  • Organic manure
