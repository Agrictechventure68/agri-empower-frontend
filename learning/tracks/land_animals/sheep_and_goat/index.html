Overview

Sheep and goat farming involves the rearing of small ruminants for meat, milk, skin, and breeding purposes. These animals are hardy, adaptable to various environments, and suitable for extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive production systems. Sheep and goats play a vital role in household income, food security, and rural livelihoods.
