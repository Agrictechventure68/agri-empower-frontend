Housing & Environment

Snails require cool, moist environments with protection from predators, direct sunlight, and flooding. Proper ventilation and soil quality are critical for survival and growth.

Feeding & Nutrition

  • Vegetables and fruits
  • Calcium sources (eggshells, limestone)
  • Formulated snail feeds

Breeding & Growth

Snails are hermaphrodites and reproduce under favorable conditions. Proper feeding and moisture enhance egg laying and hatchability.
