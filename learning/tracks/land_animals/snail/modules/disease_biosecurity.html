Common Health Challenges

  • Dehydration
  • Shell breakage
  • Fungal and bacterial infections
  • Predator attacks

Biosecurity Measures

  • Regular pen cleaning
  • Use of clean water
  • Isolation of sick snails
  • Predator control

Preventive Management

Good hygiene, proper feeding, and environmental control are the most effective methods of disease prevention in snail farming.
