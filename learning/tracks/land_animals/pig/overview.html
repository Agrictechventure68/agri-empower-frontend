Overview

Pig farming is the rearing of pigs for meat production, breeding, and commercial agribusiness purposes. Pigs are fast-growing animals with high feed conversion efficiency, making them one of the most profitable livestock enterprises when properly managed.

Pig production can be practiced on small, medium, or commercial scales using intensive or semi-intensive systems. With good housing, feeding, hygiene, and health management, pig farming provides steady income, employment, and strong market opportunities.

Major Products & Uses

  • Pork meat
  • Processed pork products (bacon, sausages)
  • Breeding stock
  • Manure for organic fertilizer
