## Foundation Level

### Basic Processing
- Simple cleaning and gutting of fish
- Sun drying or chilling for short-term storage
- Small-scale packaging

### Hygiene & Safety
- Wash hands and tools
- Avoid cross-contamination
- Keep products in cool, shaded areas

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation


## Intermediate Level

### Improved Processing Techniques
- Sorting and grading by size
- Chilling or ice storage
- Basic smoking or drying methods
- Packaging for short-distance sale

> **Curriculum Level:** intermediary 


## Advanced Level

### Commercial Processing
- Mechanized gutting, grading, and filleting
- Cold storage for extended shelf life
- Packaging for retail or wholesale

### Quality Control
- Monitoring temperature and hygiene
- Labeling and traceability

> **Curriculum Level:** Advanced


## Specialization & Enterprise Focus

### Value-Added Products
- Smoked, frozen, or filleted fish for retail
- Fish-based snacks or processed products

### Enterprise Opportunities
- Fish processing plants
- Distribution & cold-chain logistics
- Export potential

> **Curriculum Level:** Specialization
