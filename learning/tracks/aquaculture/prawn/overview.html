Overview

Prawn farming involves the controlled cultivation of freshwater or brackish water prawns for food and commercial markets. Prawns are high-value aquatic species with strong demand in local and international markets.

Importance & Opportunities

  • High market value protein
  • Export potential
  • Integrated fish–prawn farming systems

Learning Coverage

  • Production systems & management
  • Disease prevention & biosecurity
  • Processing & preservation
  • Agribusiness & markets
