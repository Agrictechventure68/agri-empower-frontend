# Production & Farm Management

## Production Systems
- Earthen and concrete ponds
- Tanks (plastic or concrete)
- Cage culture in lakes and reservoirs
- Recirculatory Aquaculture Systems (RAS)

## Foundation Level
- Site selection and pond preparation
- Stocking density for tilapia
- Basic feeding schedules
- Water quality observation

## Intermediate Level
- Monosex tilapia production
- Improved feed formulation
- Growth monitoring and grading
- Water quality management (DO, pH, temperature)

## Advanced Level
- Intensive and commercial production
- Cage culture management
- Automated feeding and aeration
- Breeding and hatchery integration

## Specialization & Enterprise Focus
- Large-scale tilapia production
- Integrated hatchery–grow-out systems
- Export-oriented production
