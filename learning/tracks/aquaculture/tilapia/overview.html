Overview

Tilapia farming involves the controlled rearing of tilapia species in ponds, tanks, cages, or recirculatory aquaculture systems. Tilapia are widely known for their fast growth, efficient feed conversion, and high consumer acceptance.

Due to their adaptability to different environments and production systems, tilapia are suitable for small-scale farmers, cooperatives, and commercial aquaculture enterprises. The species plays a key role in nutrition, employment, and agribusiness development across Africa and beyond.

Why Learn Tilapia Farming?

  • High global and local demand for tilapia
  • Short production cycle and good profitability
  • Compatible with pond, tank, and cage systems
  • Strong potential for processing and export
  • Low technical entry barrier for beginners
