Overview

Beekeeping, also known as apiculture, involves the management of honeybee colonies for the production of honey, beeswax, and other hive products. It is a low-capital, environmentally friendly enterprise that supports food security, biodiversity, and income generation.

Importance & Opportunities

  • Honey and wax production
  • Crop pollination services
  • Environmental conservation
  • Export-quality hive products

Learning Coverage

  • Colony establishment & management
  • Bee health & biosecurity
  • Honey processing & preservation
  • Agribusiness & markets
