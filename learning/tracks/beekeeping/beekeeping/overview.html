Overview

Beekeeping, also known as apiculture, is the practice of managing honeybee colonies for the production of honey, beeswax, propolis, royal jelly, and pollination services. It is one of the most environmentally sustainable agribusinesses, requiring relatively low capital while offering high ecological and economic value.

Beyond honey production, beekeeping plays a critical role in improving crop yields through pollination, supporting biodiversity, and enhancing ecosystem health. It is suitable for rural, peri-urban, and commercial farming systems.

Why Learn Beekeeping?

  • Low startup cost with scalable income potential
  • High demand for honey and bee products
  • Improves crop productivity through pollination
  • Environmentally friendly and climate-smart
  • Suitable for individuals, cooperatives, and enterprises
