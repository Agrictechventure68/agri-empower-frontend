INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Improving Productivity)
Improved Varieties & Seed Selection
Use certified hybrid or open-pollinated seeds
Select varieties resistant to common diseases
Consider market preference (size, color, shape)
Enhanced Land & Crop Management
Use raised beds with mulching
Apply compost or well-rotted manure
Maintain uniform spacing for airflow
Nutrient Management
Basal application of organic manure
Supplement with NPK fertilizers where needed
Split fertilizer application to avoid nutrient loss
Irrigation Practices
Drip or furrow irrigation
Maintain consistent soil moisture
Reduce water stress during flowering and fruiting
Curriculum Level: Intermediate
Focused on yield improvement and better farm practices.
