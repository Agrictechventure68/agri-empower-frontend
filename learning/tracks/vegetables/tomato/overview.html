Overview

Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum) is one of the most widely cultivated and consumed vegetables globally. It is used fresh, cooked, and processed into paste, puree, juice, and sauces.

Tomato farming provides quick returns, strong market demand, and employment opportunities across production, processing, and marketing. It is suitable for backyard gardens, open fields, and greenhouse systems.
