Tomato processing extends shelf life, reduces post-harvest losses, and increases farmer income by converting fresh tomatoes into stable, marketable products.

Objectives

Simple Processing Methods
1. Sun-Dried Tomatoes
Wash and slice tomatoes
Remove seeds if desired
Dry under clean sun on raised trays
Turn regularly until fully dry
Store in airtight containers
2. Tomato Paste (Traditional)
Wash and boil ripe tomatoes
Blend or mash
Cook until thick
Add small salt (optional)
Cool and store in clean containers
Hygiene Practices
Use clean water
Wash hands and utensils
Avoid processing spoiled fruits
Focus: Household preservation and waste reduction.
