Overview

Ugu (Telfairia occidentalis), commonly known as fluted pumpkin, is one of the most important leafy vegetables in West Africa. It is valued for its tender leaves, edible seeds, and medicinal properties.

The crop is fast-growing, adaptable, and suitable for backyard gardening, smallholder farms, and commercial vegetable production. Ugu plays a major role in household nutrition, income generation, and food security.
