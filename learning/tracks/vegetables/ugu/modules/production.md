## Foundation Level
Ugu is a leafy vegetable grown mainly for its leaves and seeds.
It performs well in fertile, well-drained soils with adequate moisture.

> Curriculum Level: Foundation

---

## Intermediate Level

Improved practices include proper spacing, staking of vines, and
regular harvesting to encourage leaf regeneration.

> Curriculum Level: Intermediate

---
## Advanced Level
Commercial ugu production involves intensive bed preparation,
irrigation during dry season, and planned harvest cycles.

> Curriculum Level: Advanced

---

## Specialization & Enterprise Focus
Specialized production targets urban markets, seed production,
and contract supply to restaurants and institutions.

> Curriculum Level: Specialization
