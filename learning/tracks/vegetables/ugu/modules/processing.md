At this level, learners are introduced to basic ugu processing methods aimed at reducing waste and improving household nutrition.
Processing Opportunities
Fresh leaf preparation for cooking
Simple drying for short-term preservation
Local sales of freshly processed leaves
Basic Processing Steps
Harvest fresh, healthy leaves
Remove stalks and damaged parts
Wash thoroughly with clean water
Slice or shred leaves as required
Cook or dry immediately
Basic Drying Methods
Sun-drying under shade
Use of clean mats or trays
Turning leaves regularly during drying
Hygiene & Safety
Clean hands and tools
Use potable water
Avoid drying on bare ground
Limitations at This Level
Short shelf life
Quality variation
Weather-dependent drying
Curriculum Level: Foundation
Focus: Household processing and basic preservation
