1. OVERVIEW 
Scent leaf is both a culinary herb and a medicinal plant, making it a high-demand crop across:
Local food markets
Herbal medicine industries
Cosmetic and wellness sectors
Agribusiness opportunities span production, processing, packaging, and trade.


2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner / Household Enterprise)
2.1 MARKET OPPORTUNITIES 
* Local household consumption
* Neighborhood markets
* Small restaurants and food vendors

2.2 SIMPLE BUSINESS MODELS 
* Backyard or small-plot production
* Fresh leaf sales (bundles)
* Dried leaf sales in small packs

2.3 BASIC BUSINESS SKILLS 
* Simple record keeping (expenses vs sales)
* Understanding daily pricing
* Customer relationship basics

2.4 COMMON CHALLENGES 
* Short shelf life of fresh leaves
* Price fluctuations
* Poor storage practices
Curriculum Level: Foundation


3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (SME & Cooperative-Based Agribusiness)
3.1 EXPANDED MARKET CHANNELS 
* Urban open markets
* Herbal shops and pharmacies
* Local supermarkets

3.2 VALUE ADDITION STRATEGIES 
* Dried scent leaf powder
* Branded sachets
* Mixed herbal blends

3.3 BUSINESS PLANNING 
* Production scheduling
* Cost-benefit analysis
* Packaging and labeling strategy

3.4 COOPERATIVE & GROUP MODELS 
* Farmer cooperatives
* Bulk production and marketing
* Shared processing equipment
Curriculum Level: Intermediate


4. ADVANCED LEVEL (Commercial & Supply Chain Business)
4.1 COMMERCIAL MARKET OPPORTUNITIES 
* Supply to pharmaceutical companies
* Cosmetic and soap manufacturers
* Institutional buyers

4.2 ADVANCED BUSINESS OPERATIONS 
* Contract farming arrangements
* Bulk supply agreements
* Inventory and logistics management

4.3 PRICING & PROFITABILITY 
* Cost-per-unit analysis
* Value-based pricing
* Quality-based differentiation

4.4 RISK MANAGEMENT 
* Market competition
* Regulatory compliance
* Quality control failures
Curriculum Level: Advanced


5. SPECIALIZATION LEVEL (Industrial, Export & Brand Development)
5.1 SPECIALIZED MARKET SEGMENTS 
* Essential oil export
* Organic and wellness markets
* International herbal product trade

5.2 ENTERPRISE DEVELOPMENT 
* Brand creation and trademarking
* Certification (organic, GMP, NAFDAC where required)
* Digital marketing and e-commerce

5.3 EXPORT & TRADE CONSIDERATIONS 
* Export documentation
* Quality and phytosanitary standards
* International buyer negotiations

5.4 EMPLOYMENT & INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITIES 
* Processing plant operations
* Product development roles
* Agribusiness consultancy
Curriculum Level: Specialization
