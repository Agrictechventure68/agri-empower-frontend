1. OVERVIEW 
Processing scent leaf:
Extends shelf life
Preserves medicinal and culinary value
Creates income through value-added products
Processing ranges from simple household methods to industrial-scale extraction.


2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner / Household Processing)
2.1 PROCESSING OPPORTUNITIES 
* Fresh leaf use (cooking & herbal remedies)
* Sun-dried leaves for home storage

2.2 BASIC PROCESSING STEPS 
* Harvest fresh, healthy leaves
* Wash gently with clean water
* Drain and air-dry
* Sun-dry under shade
* Store in clean containers

2.3 EQUIPMENT & TOOLS 
* Basin or bucket
* Clean water
* Tray or mat
* Shade net or clean cloth
* Airtight containers

2.4 QUALITY & SAFETY CONSIDERATIONS 
* Avoid direct sunlight to preserve aroma
* Keep processing area clean
* Prevent contamination by dust or animals
Curriculum Level: Foundation
