1. PURPOSE 
Scent leaf production aims to:
Ensure sustainable cultivation
Maximize yield for household or commercial use
Promote good farm management practices
Integrate with value-added processing and markets

2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner / Household)
2.1 PRODUCTION SYSTEMS 
* Small backyard plots
* Container or pot cultivation for urban farmers
* Rain-fed cultivation with minimal irrigation

2.2 LAND PREPARATION & PLANTING 
* Well-drained loamy soil, sunny locations
* Raised beds or flat plots
Seedlings or stem cuttings planted 20â€“30â€¯cm apart

2.3 FARM MANAGEMENT PRACTICES 
* Water moderately, avoid waterlogging
* Mulching to retain moisture
* Regular weeding and manual pest inspection

2.4 COMMON CHALLENGES 
* Pests: aphids, whiteflies
* Diseases: leaf spot, root rot
* Poor spacing and overcrowding
Curriculum Level: Foundation

3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Community / SME)
3.1 IMPROVED PRODUCTION SYSTEMS 
* Nursery propagation for uniform seedlings
* Raised beds with organic amendments
* Irrigation scheduling for peak growth

3.2 NUTRIENT & SOIL MANAGEMENT 
* Compost or organic manure application
* Mulch and cover crops to improve soil fertility
* Periodic soil testing for pH adjustment

3.3 YIELD IMPROVEMENT STRATEGIES 
* Pruning to encourage branching
* Timely harvesting for maximum leaf quality
* Pest and disease scouting using simple traps
Curriculum Level: Intermediate

4. ADVANCED LEVEL (Commercial / Industrial)
4.1 COMMERCIAL PRODUCTION SYSTEMS 
* Large-scale plots with mechanized irrigation
* Controlled spacing, raised beds, or hydroponics for urban production
* Use of certified pest-resistant varieties

4.2 FARM OPERATIONS MANAGEMENT 
* Labor scheduling for planting and harvesting
* Detailed record-keeping of inputs and yield
* Integrated pest and disease management programs

4.3 RISK MANAGEMENT 
* Climate risk mitigation (shade nets, irrigation)
* Disease outbreak monitoring
* Market-linked production planning
Curriculum Level: Advanced

5. SPECIALIZATION LEVEL (Entrepreneur / Enterprise Focus)
5.1 SPECIALIZED PRODUCTION GOALS 
* Supply for medicinal or herbal product processing
* Organic certified cultivation
* Consistent quality for export or processed products

5.2 INTEGRATION WITH PROCESSING 
* Harvesting schedule aligned with processing cycles
* Direct supply to value-added processing plants (powder, oil, dried leaves)
* Coordinated storage and transport

5.3 ENTERPRISE & EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES 
*'Establishment of processing units
* Branding and marketing of fresh or processed leaves
* Contract farming schemes for consistent supply
Curriculum Level: Specialization
