1. OVERVIEW 
Scent leaf is highly perishable. Proper post-harvest handling:
Preserves aroma and medicinal value
Reduces spoilage and losses
Improves market quality and income
This module guides learners from simple household handling to commercial storage systems.

2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner / Household Use)

2.1 HARVESTING PRACTICES 
* Harvest early in the morning or late evening
* Use clean hands or simple knives
* Harvest tender, healthy leaves

2.2 HANDLING AFTER HARVEST 
* Shake off soil gently
* Avoid washing unless necessary
* Keep leaves shaded immediately after harvest

2.3 BASIC STORAGE METHODS 
* Wrap leaves in damp cloth
* Store in cool, airy places
* Short-term storage (1â€“2 days)

2.4 LOSS REDUCTION TIPS 
* Avoid bruising
* Do not pile leaves tightly
* Use quickly after harvest
Curriculum Level: Foundation


3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Market & SME Supply)
3.1 IMPROVED HARVESTING TECHNIQUES 
* Harvest with clean, sharp tools
* Sort damaged leaves at harvest point
* Harvest according to market demand

3.2 POST-HARVEST HANDLING 
* Gentle washing with clean water
* Draining excess moisture
* Bundling in small, uniform quantities

3.3 STORAGE IMPROVEMENTS 
* Use ventilated baskets or crates
* Temporary storage in cool rooms
* Shelf life extension to 3â€“5 days

3.4 QUALITY MAINTENANCE 
* Regular inspection during storage
* Remove spoiled leaves immediately
Curriculum Level: Intermediate


4. ADVANCED LEVEL (Commercial & Processing Supply)
4.1 COMMERCIAL HARVEST PLANNING 
* Harvest scheduling aligned with buyers
* Use of clean plastic crates
* Organized transport logistics

4.2 ADVANCED STORAGE TECHNIQUES 
* Refrigerated storage (short-term)
* Modified atmosphere packaging
* Humidity control to preserve freshness

4.3 LOSS REDUCTION STRATEGIES 
* Cold chain management
* Rapid transportation
* Temperature monitoring
Curriculum Level: Advanced


5. SPECIALIZATION LEVEL (Processing & Supply Chain Focus)
5.1 PROCESSING-READY HANDLING 
* Harvest maturity optimized for drying or extraction
* Bulk handling systems
* Sorting by quality grades

5.2 STORAGE FOR INDUSTRIAL USE 
* Pre-drying shade houses
* Controlled drying environments
* Bulk dried-leaf storage facilities

5.3 ENTERPRISE OPPORTUNITIES 
* Fresh herb supply businesses
* Dried herb packaging
* Herbal medicine raw material supply
Curriculum Level: Specialization
