Vegetable Crop (Leafy, Medicinal & Aromatic Herb)

Overview

Scent leaf is a widely cultivated aromatic vegetable used in cooking, traditional medicine, and natural pest control. It is valued for its strong flavor, medicinal properties, and ease of cultivation.

The crop is hardy, fast-growing, and suitable for backyard gardens, urban farming, and commercial leafy vegetable production. Its year-round demand makes it a reliable income source.
