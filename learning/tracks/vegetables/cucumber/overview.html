Overview

Cucumber is a fast-growing vegetable crop cultivated primarily for its fresh fruits, which are consumed raw, cooked, or processed. It is widely grown under open-field and protected (greenhouse) systems.

Due to its short growth cycle, high market demand, and adaptability, cucumber farming is suitable for smallholders, urban farmers, and commercial vegetable enterprises.
