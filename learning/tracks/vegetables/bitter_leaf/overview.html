Vegetable Crop (Leafy & Medicinal Vegetable)

Overview

Bitterleaf is a widely consumed leafy vegetable in Africa, valued for its nutritional, medicinal, and economic importance. It is commonly used in soups and herbal remedies.

The crop is hardy, perennial, and suitable for backyard gardens, smallholder farms, and commercial leafy vegetable production. Its year-round harvest potential makes it a reliable income source.
