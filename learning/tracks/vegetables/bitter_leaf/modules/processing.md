1. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING BITTER LEAF 
Processing bitter leaf helps to:
Extend shelf life
Reduce bitterness to acceptable levels
Improve convenience for consumers
Create value-added products
Increase farmer and processor income

2. FORMS OF BITTER LEAF PROCESSING 
Bitter leaf can be processed into:
Fresh washed leaves
Blanched leaves
Dried leaves (whole or powdered)
Packaged ready-to-cook leaves
Herbal/medicinal products

3. PROCESSING STAGES (General Flow)
Harvesting
Sorting & washing
Debittering
Blanching (optional)
Drying or packaging
Storage & distribution

7. PACKAGING & STORAGE
