Vegetable Crop (Root Vegetable)

Overview

Carrot is a cool- and warm-season root vegetable cultivated for its edible taproot, rich in vitamins and dietary fiber. It is widely consumed fresh, cooked, or processed into juices and packaged products.

Due to its short production cycle and strong market demand, carrot farming provides quick income opportunities for smallholders and commercial vegetable producers.
