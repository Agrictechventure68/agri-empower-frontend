Pest, Disease & Biosecurity

← Back to Okra Modules ← Back to Vegetables

Foundation Level

  • Common pests: Aphids, Whiteflies, Caterpillars
  • Common diseases: Powdery mildew, Fusarium wilt, Leaf spots
  • Soil: Well-drained sandy loam; avoid waterlogging
  • Basic control: Remove infested plants, rotate crops

Intermediate Level

  • Field scouting for early pest detection
  • Neem-based or organic insecticides
  • Prune infected plant parts; grow resistant varieties
  • Soil fertility: compost, farmyard manure, mulching

Advanced Level

  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): cultural, biological, chemical
  • Pheromone/sticky traps for monitoring
  • Disease calendar & lab diagnostics
  • Crop rotation and deep tillage for soil health

Specialization

  • Farm-specific pest control programs
  • Lab-based disease diagnostics
  • Precision fertilization & soil mapping
  • Smart scouting and remote sensing for pest/disease
