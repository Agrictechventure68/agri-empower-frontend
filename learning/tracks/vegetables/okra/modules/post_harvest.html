Post-Harvest Handling & Storage

← Back to Okra Modules ← Back to Vegetables

Foundation Level

  • Harvest tender pods at correct maturity
  • Bundle and handle gently
  • Short-term storage under cool conditions
  • Sun-drying to reduce moisture for longer shelf-life

Intermediate Level

  • Use small-scale threshers or manual methods
  • Sorting and grading pods for uniformity
  • Improved storage in ventilated bins
  • Inspect for pests and mold regularly

Advanced Level

  • Coordinate harvest for commercial buyers
  • Control moisture with mechanical or solar dryers
  • Pest-proof storage facilities
  • Batch tracking and quality inspection

Specialization

  • Processing-ready storage for premium markets
  • Bulk storage with temperature/humidity control
  • Integration with value chain partners (processors, wholesalers)
  • Training and employment in post-harvest management
