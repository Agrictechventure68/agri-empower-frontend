Overview

Okra is a widely cultivated vegetable crop valued for its tender pods, nutritional benefits, and adaptability to diverse agro-ecological zones. It is consumed fresh, dried, or processed into soups and sauces.

Okra farming is popular among smallholder farmers due to its short maturity period, low input requirements, and strong local market demand. It can be grown year-round under rain-fed or irrigated systems.
