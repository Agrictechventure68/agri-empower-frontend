Overview

Onion is a widely cultivated bulb vegetable valued for its culinary, nutritional, and medicinal importance. It is a staple ingredient in households and food industries across the world.

Onion farming offers strong income potential due to high demand, storability, and suitability for both smallholder and commercial production systems.
