Overview

Pepper (Capsicum spp.) is a widely cultivated vegetable crop valued for its pungency, flavor, and nutritional benefits. It is consumed fresh, dried, powdered, or processed into sauces and pastes.

Pepper farming is profitable due to strong market demand, multiple varieties, and suitability for both rainy and dry-season cultivation. It supports smallholder farmers and commercial agribusiness ventures.
