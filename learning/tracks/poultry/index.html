Overview

Poultry farming involves the rearing of birds for meat, eggs, and value-added products. Due to differences in growth cycles, housing, feeding, and market models, Agri_Empower structures poultry learning by species for clarity and practical application.

Learning Levels

Each poultry species is taught across four progressive levels:

  • Foundation: Basics and setup
  • Intermediate: Productivity and control
  • Advanced: Optimization and scaling
  • Specialization: Breeding, processing, or niche markets
