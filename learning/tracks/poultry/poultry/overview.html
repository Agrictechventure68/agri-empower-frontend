Overview

Poultry farming involves the rearing of birds such as chickens, turkeys, ducks, and guinea fowls for meat and egg production. It is one of the fastest growing livestock enterprises due to its quick returns, high market demand, and adaptability to small, medium, and commercial-scale operations.
