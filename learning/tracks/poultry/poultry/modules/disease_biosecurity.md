## Foundation Level

### Common Poultry Diseases

- Fowl pox

### Basic Biosecurity Measures
- Restrict visitors to poultry house
- Wash hands before handling birds
- Avoid contact with wild birds

### Hygiene Practices
- Daily cleaning of feeders and drinkers
- Removing litter regularly
- Simple waste disposal

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation


## Intermediate Level

### Disease Prevention
- Vaccination schedules for layers and broilers
- Use of disinfectants in poultry houses
- Isolation of sick birds

### Enhanced Biosecurity
- Controlled access to farm
- Footbaths and protective clothing
- Cleaning transport equipment

### Monitoring & Record Keeping
- Track disease occurrence
- Document treatments and outcomes

> **Curriculum Level:** Intermediate


## Advanced Level

### Integrated Health Management
- Combination of vaccination, nutrition, and environmental control
- Laboratory-based disease diagnostics
- Health audits and regular veterinary consultation

### Risk Management
- Early warning systems for outbreaks
- Farm layout to prevent cross-infection
- Pest and rodent control

### Worker Safety & Training
- Proper handling of vaccines and medications
- Training staff on disease recognition

> **Curriculum Level:** Advanced
