## Foundation Level

### Production Systems
- **Backyard systems:** Small flocks for household use
- **Semi-intensive systems:** Small commercial operations with simple housing
- **Intensive systems:** Larger flocks, controlled housing and feeding

### Categories
- **Layers:** For egg production
- **Broilers:** For meat production

### Basic Farm Management
- Daily feeding and water provision
- Simple record keeping
- Observation for sick birds

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation

## Intermediate Level

### Improved Production Systems
- Optimized stocking density
- Better housing design for ventilation and hygiene
- Routine vaccination schedules

### Categories Management
- Layers: Lighting management, nesting boxes, egg collection
- Broilers: Feed conversion monitoring, growth tracking

### Health & Environment
- Clean water and feed storage
- Isolation of new birds to prevent disease
- Basic biosecurity protocols

> **Curriculum Level:** Intermediate


## Advanced Level

### Commercial Production
- Full-scale layer or broiler farms
- Automated feeding, watering, and climate control
- Batch management and rotation

### Advanced Management Practices
- Nutrition optimization based on growth stage
- Record keeping for performance metrics
- Labor management

### Health & Risk Management
- Disease surveillance and vaccination programs
- Emergency response planning
- Pest and predator control

> **Curriculum Level:** Advanced


## Specialization & Enterprise Focus

### Layer Operations
- Egg collection, grading, and packaging
- Value-added products (boiled eggs, powdered eggs)

### Broiler Operations
- Meat processing for market
- Value chain integration with processors

### Enterprise Opportunities
- Hatchery operations
- Feed production
- Contract farming with agribusinesses

> **Curriculum Level:** Specialization
