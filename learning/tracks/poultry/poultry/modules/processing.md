## Foundation Level

### Basic Processing
- Egg collection and simple cleaning for layers
- Slaughtering of broilers on a small scale
- Manual cleaning and basic packaging

### Hygiene & Safety
- Wash hands before handling eggs or meat
- Use clean knives and surfaces
- Store products in cool, shaded conditions

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation


## Intermediate Level

### Improved Processing Techniques
- Sorting, grading, and packaging eggs
- Slaughtering in hygienic conditions
- Chilling meat in simple refrigerators
- Basic shelf-life awareness

### Waste Management
- Proper disposal of feathers, offal, and waste water
- Composting or feeding scraps to other livestock

> **Curriculum Level:** Intermediate


## Advanced Level

### Commercial Processing
- Mechanized egg collection and grading
- Small-scale broiler slaughterhouse operations
- Cold storage for extended shelf life

### Quality Assurance
- Monitoring temperatures
- Packaging for retail or industrial supply
- Traceability for products

> **Curriculum Level:** Advanced


## Specialization & Enterprise Focus

### Value-Added Products

### Enterprise Opportunities

- Egg processing plants
- Distribution & cold-chain logistics

> **Curriculum Level:** Specialization
