Overview

Sorghum is a hardy cereal crop well adapted to hot and dry climates. It is widely grown for food, livestock feed, brewing, and industrial raw materials.

Due to its drought tolerance and versatility, sorghum plays a key role in climate-smart agriculture and food security, especially in semi-arid regions.
