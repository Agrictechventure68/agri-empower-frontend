Pests, Diseases & Biosecurity

Common Diseases

  • Cocoyam leaf blight
  • Root rot diseases
  • Fungal leaf spots

Major Pests

  • Taro beetles
  • Nematodes

Control & Biosecurity

  • Use of disease-free planting materials
  • Good drainage and field sanitation
  • Crop rotation and resistant varieties
  • Integrated pest management (IPM)
