Processing & Equipment Requirements (All Levels)


1. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner)

COMMON COCOYAM PROCESSED PRODUCTS 
* Boiled cocoyam
* Pounded cocoyam (fufu)
* Dried cocoyam chips
* Cocoyam flour (basic)

BASIC PROCESSING STEPS 
* Peeling with knife
* Washing with clean water
* Cooking or drying
* Milling (for flour)

BASIC EQUIPMENT 
* Knife / peeler
* Buckets
* Cooking pots
* Firewood or gas stove
* Drying mats or trays
LEARNING GOAL: Household food processing and reduced spoilage


2. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL 

IMPROVED PROCESSED PRODUCTS 
* High-quality cocoyam flour
* Dried chips for storage
*'Composite flour (cocoyam + cereals)

IMPROVED PROCESSING FLOW 
* Sorting & cleaning
* Peeling and slicing uniformly
* Blanching (optional)
* Solar drying
* Milling and sieving
* Packaging

EQUIPMENT REQUIRED 
* Stainless steel knives
* Slicers (manual)
* Solar dryer
* Hammer mill or plate mill
* Weighing scale
* Sealing machine (manual)
OUTCOME: Improved quality, longer shelf life


3. ADVANCED LEVEL 

VALUE-ADDED PRODUCTS 
* Industrial-grade cocoyam flour
* Instant cocoyam swallow
* Cocoyam starch
* Baby food blends

ADVANCED PROCESSING STEPS 
* Controlled drying
* Fine milling
* Moisture testing
* Hygienic packaging

EQUIPMENT 
* Mechanical slicer
* Cabinet dryer / tunnel dryer
* Electric milling machine
* Moisture meter
* Packaging machine
* Generator / inverter
FOCUS: Standardization & food safety


4. SPECIALIZATION / COMMERCIAL LEVEL 

INDUSTRIAL PROCESSING 
* Large-scale flour production
* Export-grade cocoyam starch
* Institutional food supply
* Industrial Equipment
* Washing machines
* Conveyor slicers
* Industrial dryers
* Cyclone mills
* Automatic packaging lines
* Quality control lab tools

REGULATORY CONSIDERATIONS 
* NAFDAC registration
* Food safety certification (HACCP)
* Batch tracking and labeling
