Sweet Potato

Nutritious root crop for food security, income, and processing

Category

Food Crop (Root & Tuber)

Overview

Sweet potato is a fast-growing, drought-tolerant root crop widely cultivated for its nutritious tubers and edible leaves. It plays a critical role in food security, household nutrition, and income generation, especially among smallholder farmers.

The crop is rich in carbohydrates, vitamins, and antioxidants, making it valuable for human consumption, livestock feed, and industrial processing. Sweet potato adapts well to diverse agro-ecological zones and short production cycles.
