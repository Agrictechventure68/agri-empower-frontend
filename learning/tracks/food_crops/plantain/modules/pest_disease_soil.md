FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner)

COMMON PESTS 
Aphids, nematodes, banana weevil
Leaf damage, reduced growth, stunted suckers

COMMON DISEASES 
Black Sigatoka, Bacterial Wilt

SYMPTOMS: leaf spotting, wilting, fruit damage

SOIL HEALTH BASICS 
* Well-drained fertile soils preferred
* Maintain organic matter content

BASIC MANAGEMENT PRACTICES 
* Remove affected leaves and plants
* Mulch around base to retain moisture
* Plant disease-free suckers
Curriculum Level: Foundation


INTERMEDIATE LEVEL 

PEST MANAGEMENT PRACTICES 
* Regular monitoring for banana weevil and aphids
* Biological pest controls (e.g., predatory insects)
* Avoid monocropping to reduce pest buildup

DISEASE PREVENTION STRATEGIES 
* Plant resistant varieties
* Proper spacing for air circulation
* Sanitation and field hygiene

SOIL FERTILITY MANAGEMENT 
* Application of compost, manure, or balanced NPK
* Soil testing to guide fertilizer use
* Mulching to conserve moisture
Curriculum Level: Intermediate


ADVANCED LEVEL 

INTEGRATED PEST MANAGEMENT (IPM)
* Combine cultural, biological, and chemical methods
* Monitor and forecast pest/disease outbreaks
* Use traps, pheromones, and beneficial insects

DISEASE SURVEILLANCE 
* Early detection and rapid response protocols
* Collaboration with agricultural extension agents

ADVANCED SOIL MANAGEMENT 
* Soil amendments based on comprehensive testing
* Cover cropping and erosion control
* Precision nutrient management
Curriculum Level: Advanced


SPECIALIZATION & DIAGNOSTIC FOCUS 
* Specialized Pest Control
* Large-scale commercial pest strategies
* Field and laboratory pest identification
* Integrated resistant variety deployment

DISEASE DIAGNOSTICS 
* Laboratory testing for pathogens
* Data-driven disease management plans

SOIL HEALTH SYSTEMS 
* Mapping soil fertility for plantations
* Sustainable land management plans
* Integration with crop rotation and organic amendments
Curriculum Level: Specialization
