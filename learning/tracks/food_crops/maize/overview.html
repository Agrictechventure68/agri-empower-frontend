Food Crop (Cereal)

Overview

Maize is one of the most widely cultivated cereal crops in the world and a major staple food in Africa. It is valued for its versatility, high yield potential, and wide range of food, feed, and industrial uses.

The crop is adaptable to different agro-ecological zones and production systems, from smallholder rain-fed farms to large-scale commercial operations. Maize plays a critical role in food security, livestock feed supply, and agro-industrial development.

Major Uses

  • Human food (boiled, roasted, pap, flour, meal)
  • Livestock and poultry feed
  • Starch and ethanol production
  • Industrial raw material (brewing, bio-products)
