## Foundation Level

### Harvesting Practices
Cassava should be harvested at the right maturity to ensure good quality
and reduce post-harvest losses.

### Handling After Harvest
Avoid mechanical damage during harvesting and transport.
Roots should be processed or stored promptly after harvest.

### Basic Storage Methods
Short-term storage methods include keeping roots in cool, shaded conditions
or covering them with moist materials.

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation
