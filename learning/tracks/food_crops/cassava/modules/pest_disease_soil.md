## Foundation Level

### Common Pests
Cassava pests include mealybugs, green mites, and termites.
These pests reduce plant vigor and yield if not controlled early.

### Common Diseases
Cassava mosaic disease and cassava brown streak disease are common.
Using healthy planting materials helps reduce disease spread.

### Basic Soil Management
Cassava grows best in well-drained soils.
Avoid waterlogged areas and rotate crops to maintain soil health.

> **Curriculum Level:** Foundation

## Intermediate Level

### Pest Management Practices
- Regular field scouting
- Removal of infested plants
- Use of recommended biological or chemical controls

### Disease Prevention Strategies
- Planting resistant varieties
- Proper field sanitation
- Timely removal of diseased plants

### Soil Fertility Management
- Application of organic manure
- Use of soil test results to guide fertilization

> **Curriculum Level:** Intermediate


## Advanced Level

### Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

- Monitoring pest populations before intervention

### Disease Surveillance
- Early detection systems
- Collaboration with extension services

### Advanced Soil Management
