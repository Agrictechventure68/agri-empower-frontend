Pests, Diseases & Biosecurity

Major Cassava Diseases

  • Cassava Mosaic Disease (CMD)
  • Cassava Brown Streak Disease
  • Bacterial blight

Common Pests

  • Cassava mealybug
  • Green spider mites

Biosecurity & Control Measures

  • Use of disease-free planting materials
  • Field sanitation and crop rotation
  • Early disease detection and removal
  • Integrated pest management (IPM)
