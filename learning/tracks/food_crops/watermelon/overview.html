Watermelon

High-value vegetable crop with fast market turnover

Category

Food Crop (Vegetable / Cucurbit)

Overview

Watermelon is a fast-growing vegetable crop cultivated mainly for its juicy, sweet fruits. It is highly demanded across urban and rural markets due to its refreshing taste, high water content, and nutritional benefits.

The crop has a short maturity period, making it attractive for dry-season farming and commercial vegetable production. With proper spacing, pest management, and market timing, watermelon farming offers quick income generation and high profit margins.

Major Uses

  • Fresh fruit consumption
  • Fruit salads and beverages
  • Juice extraction
  • Seed snacks and oil (in some regions)
