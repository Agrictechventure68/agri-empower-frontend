1. INTRODUCTION 
Effective pest, disease, and soil health management is critical for achieving high yields, quality fruits, and sustainable watermelon production. This module equips learners at all levels with preventive, corrective, and integrated management strategies.


2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner Farmers)
2.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Identify common watermelon pests and diseases
* Understand basic soil health practices
* Apply simple prevention methods

2.2 COMMON PESTS (Beginner Level)
 a. Aphids

SIGNS: Curled leaves, sticky substance (honeydew)
EFFECT: Weak plants, disease transmission
BASIC CONTROL 
* Hand removal
* Neem leaf extract spray
*Remove heavily infested plants

b. Beetles

SIGNS: Holes on leaves
EFFECT: Reduced photosynthesis
BASIC CONTROL 
* Handpicking
* Ash or pepper solution around plants

2.3 COMMON DISEASES (Beginner Level)
a. Powdery Mildew
SIGNS: White powder on leaves
CONTROL:
* Improve spacing
* Remove infected leaves

b. Damping-off
SIGNS: Seedlings collapse
CONTROL:
* Avoid waterlogging
* Use well-drained soil

2.4 BASIC SOIL HEALTH PRACTICES 
* Use loose, well-drained soil
* Add compost or farmyard manure
* Avoid continuous cropping
Outcome: Beginner farmer can recognize and reduce basic pest and disease problems.


3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Improved Management)
3.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Apply integrated pest and disease management
* Improve soil fertility and structure
* Reduce yield losses

3.2 KEY PESTS (Intermediate Level)
a. Fruit Fly
DAMAGE: Larvae feed inside fruits
Management:
* Destroy affected fruits
* Use bait traps

b. Thrips
DAMAGE: Leaf distortion, poor fruit set
MANAGEMENT:
* Neem-based insecticides
* Mulching to reduce breeding

3.3 MAJOR DISEASES (Intermediate Level)
a. Fusarium Wilt
SIGNS: Yellowing, wilting, plant death
CONTROL:
* Crop rotation (2‚Äì3 years)
* Resistant varieties

b. Downy Mildew
SIGNS: Yellow patches on leaves
CONTROL:
* Proper spacing
* Prevent overhead irrigation

3.4 SOIL HEALTH IMPROVEMENT 
* Apply organic manure before planting
* Use cover crops
* Maintain soil moisture balance
Outcome: Learner reduces pest pressure and improves soil productivity.
