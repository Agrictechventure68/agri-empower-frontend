1. INTRODUCTION 
Watermelon processing transforms fresh fruits into value-added products, reduces post-harvest losses, and opens new income streams. This module introduces learners to simple to advanced processing techniques, equipment requirements, and quality standards.


2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner Processing)
2.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Understand basic watermelon processing concepts
* Learn simple home-scale processing
* Reduce waste from unsold fruits

2.2 BASIC Watermelon PROCESSING PRODUCTS 
At beginner level, processing focuses on fresh consumption and simple transformations:
* Fresh watermelon slices
* Watermelon juice
* Watermelon fruit salad
* Watermelon popsicles (ice treats)

2.3 SIMPLE PROCESSING STEPS 
* Wash fruits thoroughly
* Cut with clean knives
* Remove seeds (optional)
* Serve fresh or refrigerate

3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Small-Scale Processing)
3.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Process watermelon into shelf-stable products
*'Package products for sale
* Improve product quality and consistency

3.2 INTERMEDIATE PROCESSING PRODUCTS 
* Bottled watermelon juice
* Watermelon smoothie blends
* Watermelon concentrate
* Dried watermelon slices

3.3 PROCESSING TECHNIQUES 
a) Juice & Beverage Processing
Extract juice using blender or extractor
Filter pulp if required
Pasteurize lightly (low heat)
Cool and bottle

b) Drying
* Slice fruit thinly
* Sun-dry or use cabinet dryer
* Store in airtight containers
