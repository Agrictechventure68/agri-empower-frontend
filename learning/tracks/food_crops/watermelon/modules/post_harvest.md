2.2 HARVESTING PRACTICES

* Yellow spot at ground contact
* Dry tendril near fruit
* Dull (not shiny) skin

* Store in cool, shaded, well-ventilated areas
* Keep fruits off the ground (use pallets or straw)
* Avoid direct sunlight
