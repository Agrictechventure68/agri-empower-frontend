Overview

Banana is a major tropical food and fruit crop cultivated for household consumption, fresh markets, and processing industries. It is valued for its high energy content, year-round production, and strong market demand.

Banana farming supports smallholder farmers and commercial plantations. With good agronomic practices, bananas offer quick returns, employment opportunities, and strong agribusiness potential.
