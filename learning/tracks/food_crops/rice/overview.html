Food Crop (Cereal Grain)

Overview

Rice is one of the world’s most important staple crops and a major source of calories for millions of people. In Africa, rice production plays a critical role in food security, job creation, and import substitution.

Rice can be grown under upland, lowland, irrigated, and floodplain systems. With improved varieties, good water management, and mechanization, rice farming offers high yield potential and strong agribusiness opportunities.
