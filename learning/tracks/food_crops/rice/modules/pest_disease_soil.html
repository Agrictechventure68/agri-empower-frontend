Pests, Diseases & Biosecurity

Major Pests

  • Rice stem borers
  • Rice weevils
  • Armyworms

Common Diseases

  • Rice blast
  • Bacterial leaf blight
  • Sheath blight

Biosecurity Measures

  • Use of certified seeds
  • Proper water management
  • Crop rotation and field sanitation
  • Integrated pest management (IPM)
