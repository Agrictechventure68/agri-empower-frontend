1. INTRODUCTION 
Yam processing transforms fresh tubers into more stable, marketable, and higher-value products. Processing reduces post-harvest losses, extends shelf life, and creates employment opportunities along the value chain.


2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner Farmers)
2.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Understand basic yam processing
* Produce simple yam products
* Use basic tools safely

2.2 COMMON SIMPLE YAM PRODUCTS 
* Boiled yam
* Pounded yam (iyan)
* Roasted yam
* Yam flour (local methods)

2.3 BASIC PROCESSING STEPS 
* Peeling
* Washing
* Cutting or slicing
* Cooking or drying

2.4 BASIC EQUIPMENT & TOOLS 
* Knife or cutlass
* Basin and clean water
* Cooking pots
* Firewood or gas stove
* Mats for sun drying

2.5 HYGIENE & SAFETY 
* Wash hands before processing
* Use clean water
* Avoid processing rotten tubers
Curriculum Level: Foundation


3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Improved Processing Techniques)
3.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Improve product quality
* Reduce processing losses
* Introduce simple mechanization

3.2 IMPROVED YAM PRODUCTS 
* Dried yam chips
* Improved yam flour (elubo)
* Semi-processed yam slices

3.3 IMPROVED PROCESSING TECHNIQUES 
* Uniform slicing for even drying
* Controlled sun drying or shade drying
* Sorting before processing

3.4 INTERMEDIATE EQUIPMENT 
* Manual slicers
* Improved drying racks
* Mortar and pestle or small mills
* Solar dryers (basic models)

3.5 QUALITY IMPROVEMENT 
* Proper drying to prevent mould
* Clean packaging materials
* Labeling for local markets
* Curriculum Level: Intermediate


4. ADVANCED LEVEL (Commercial Processing)
4.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Produce for urban and regional markets
* Improve efficiency and consistency
* Meet quality standards

4.2 ADVANCED YAM PRODUCTS 
* Industrial yam flour
* Instant pounded yam powder
* Pre-packaged yam chips

4.3 ADVANCED PROCESSING OPERATIONS 
* Mechanical peeling and slicing
* Controlled drying systems
* Milling and sieving

4.4 ADVANCED EQUIPMENT 
* Mechanical peelers
* Industrial slicers
* Hammer mills or flour mills
* Mechanical or solar dryers
* Sealing and packaging machines

4.5 QUALITY CONTROL 
* Moisture content testing
* Product grading
* Batch tracking
Curriculum Level: Advanced


5. SPECIALIZATION LEVEL (Enterprise & Industrial Processing)
5.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Establish yam processing enterprises
* Integrate processing into value chains
* Target export and institutional markets

5.2 SPECIALIZED YAM PRODUCTS 
* Export-grade yam flour
* Instant pounded yam brands
* Composite flour blends

5.3 INDUSTRIAL EQUIPMENT & SYSTEMS 
* Automated processing lines
* Large-scale dryers
* Quality testing equipment

5.4 STANDARDS & CERTIFICATION 
* Food safety standards (NAFDAC)
* Hygiene certification
* Packaging and labeling compliance

5.5 ENTERPRISE & EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES 
* Yam processing plant operator
* Equipment maintenance services
* Packaging and branding businesses
* Distribution and logistics services

6. SUSTAINABILITY & INNOVATION

* Waste utilization (peels for feed or compost)

Effective yam processing:
* Adds value
* Creates jobs
* Improves food availability
* Increases farmer income
