1. INTRODUCTION 
Yam agribusiness involves understanding the market, creating value, linking production with buyers, and building profitable enterprises. Proper business skills are crucial for sustainability and scaling.


2. FOUNDATION LEVEL (Beginner Farmers)
2.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Understand basic yam marketing concepts
* Keep simple business records
* Identify local market opportunities

2.2 MARKET AWARENESS 
* Local markets (villages and towns)
* Understanding demand and peak selling periods
* Awareness of buyer preferences

2.3 BASIC BUSINESS SKILLS 
* Recording sales and purchases
* Tracking costs vs. income
* Identifying simple business risks

2.4 SIMPLE VALUE ADDITION 
* Sorting and grading yams
* Packaging for small-scale sales
* Basic branding ideas
Curriculum Level: Foundation


3. INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Improved Agribusiness)
3.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Optimize profits
* Access regional buyers
* Plan business operations efficiently

3.2 BUSINESS PLANNING 
* Crop budgeting and production cost calculation
* Planning harvest to meet market demand
* Managing working capital

3.3 MARKET ACCESS & NETWORKING 
* Farmer cooperatives and groups
* Local wholesalers and processors
* Small-scale retail distribution

3.4 INTERMEDIATE VALUE CHAIN PRACTICES 
* Sorting, grading, and cleaning for better market value
* Basic packaging and labeling
*:Linking production to small processors
Curriculum Level: Intermediate


4. ADVANCED LEVEL (Commercial Agribusiness)
4.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Operate large-scale yam businesses
* Integrate with urban and regional markets
* Mitigate commercial risks

4.2 COMMERCIAL AGRIBUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES 
* Selling to food processors and industrial buyers
* Bulk trading for export markets
* Contract farming arrangements

4.3 ADVANCED MARKET PRACTICES 
* Market research and pricing strategies
* Branding and product differentiation
* Quality assurance for bulk sales

4.4 RISK MANAGEMENT 
* Price volatility
* Pest and disease impacts on supply
* Logistics and storage challenges
Curriculum Level: Advanced


5. SPECIALIZATION LEVEL (Enterprise & Value Chain Integration)
5.1 LEARNING OBJECTIVES 
* Build industrial-scale yam enterprises
* Optimize value chain linkages
* Explore export and high-value markets

5.2 SPECIALIZED MARKET OPPORTUNITIES 
* Industrial yam flour contracts
* Instant pounded yam and packaged products
* Composite flour blends for institutional buyers

5.3 VALUE CHAIN OPTIMIZATION 
* Integration with farmers, cooperatives, and processors
* Logistics and cold storage solutions
* Branding and export compliance

5.4 ENTERPRISE & EMPLOYMENT CREATION 
* Processing plant management
* Distribution and logistics services
* Packaging and marketing businesses
* Agro-input supply for yam farmers
Curriculum Level: Specialization

6. Sustainability & Strategic Growth
* Reducing post-harvest losses through business planning
* Using technology for market intelligence
* Developing long-term supply contracts

7. SUMMARY 
A strong yam agribusiness:
* Improves farmer income
* Reduces waste
* Increases market access
*'Supports industrial and export growth
