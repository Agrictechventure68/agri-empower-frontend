Food Crop (Legume)

Overview

Beans are an important leguminous food crop cultivated primarily for their protein-rich grains. They play a vital role in household nutrition, income generation, and sustainable farming systems.

As nitrogen-fixing crops, beans improve soil fertility and are commonly integrated into crop rotations and mixed farming systems. They are widely consumed locally and have strong regional market demand.
