Food Crop (Cereal Grain)

Overview

Millet is a drought-tolerant cereal crop cultivated widely in arid and semi-arid regions. It is valued for its short growing cycle, low input requirements, and high nutritional content.

Millet plays a critical role in food security, climate-smart agriculture, and rural livelihoods. It is consumed directly as food and processed into flour, porridge, beverages, and livestock feed.
