Medicinal & Aromatic Crop (Herb)

Overview

Lemongrass (Cymbopogon citratus) is a perennial aromatic herb cultivated primarily for its essential oil, medicinal properties, and use in herbal teas and wellness products. It is widely valued for its lemon scent, antimicrobial activity, and therapeutic benefits.

Beyond household use, lemongrass plays an important role in the pharmaceutical, cosmetic, food, and aromatherapy industries. Its adaptability, low input requirement, and multiple value chains make it a strategic crop for smallholders and agribusiness investors.

Importance & Uses

  • Herbal teas and functional beverages
  • Essential oil for aromatherapy and cosmetics
  • Medicinal preparations and traditional remedies
  • Natural insect repellent and antimicrobial agent
  • Income diversification for farmers
